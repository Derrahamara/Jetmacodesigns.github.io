ACCESS 24

Creating an Accessible 24-Hour Service Network in Lagos

VIEW RESEARCH CASE STUDIES VIEW PROJECT

About Project

The "Creating an Accessible 24-Hour Service Network" project addresses the challenges of service delivery in Lagos, Nigeria, driven by rapid urbanization and population growth. It aims to improve access to essential services through innovative technology, like mobile apps that provide real-time information. Key motivations include the urgent need for better accessibility, the potential of technology to bridge service gaps, and a commitment to community well-being. The project emphasizes a tailored approach that considers local context and infrastructural limitations.

Objectives

The project objectives are to assess service gaps in Lagos, develop user-friendly apps for real-time service updates, collaborate with government and community organizations, and implement pilot programs to evaluate solutions. Ultimately, the goal is to create a more inclusive service network that ensures all residents have access to essential services at any time.

BOSS PAY

Boosting Employee Financial Wellness

VIEW RESEARCH CASE STUDIES VIEW PROJECT

About Project

Boosting Employee Financial Wellness through Earned Wage Access (EWA) in Nigeria The "Boosting Employee Financial Wellness" project aims to implement Earned Wage Access (EWA) in Nigeria to alleviate financial stress among employees facing high inflation and economic challenges. Many Nigerians live paycheck to paycheck, leading to decreased productivity and job satisfaction. EWA allows employees to access earned wages before payday, helping them manage cash flow and reduce reliance on high-interest loans.

Objectives

The project objectives are to assess financial stress by evaluating the challenges faced by employees and their impact on productivity, implement Earned Wage Access (EWA) solutions within payroll systems to facilitate immediate wage access, enhance financial wellness by providing tools and resources for managing finances, and evaluate the impact of EWA on employee satisfaction and productivity
